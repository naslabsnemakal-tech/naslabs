About NAS Labs

NAS Labs is an independent AI dataset development organization focused on building real-world industrial video datasets for machine learning, computer vision, and automation systems. We specialize in ethically collected, structured, and scalable industrial data solutions prepared for enterprise AI training.

Our Dataset Solutions

Annotation & Structuring

Domain-specific data capture, scalable recording setups, and tailored AI dataset production based on enterprise and marketplace requirements.
